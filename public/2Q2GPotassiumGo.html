Minecraft Building Guide

A guide on how to Build in Minecraft

Check out the homepage!

Minecraft as a game has a lot to offer. One aspect that many players focus on is building. Laid on the side is a table containing most (if not all) Minecraft Blocks obtainable in Survival Mode for you to be able to build with. These block families have been separated by color and have been generalized per group.

Now, why are they separated by color? This is because in Minecraft, it is recommended to use many different blocks together regardless of texture or difference in block family because they have similar colors or colors that have a good contrast/relationship. Many professional builders opt to use unique block palettes and choices to make new and better builds.

Another way of getting better at building is following creators! Below are a bunch of videos that I'd recommend watching if you want to get better at building.
